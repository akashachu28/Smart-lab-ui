Executive Summary
AISense is an enterprise-grade AI Security, Governance, and Defense Platform designed to secure datasets, models, AI pipelines, GenAI applications, AI agents, and production deployments.
The platform provides end-to-end security coverage across the AI lifecycle, including dataset validation, model vulnerability assessment, adversarial testing, runtime protection, AI governance, compliance management, and automated AI red teaming.
Unlike traditional AI security solutions focused solely on LLMs, AegisAI provides unified protection for Computer Vision, Speech AI, Generative AI, Agentic Systems, and traditional machine learning workloads.
Product Vision
Enable organizations to safely build, deploy, govern, and operate AI systems at enterprise scale.
